Spaghetti Carbonara


  • Prep Time: 15mins
  • Cook Time: 20mins
  • Total Time: 35mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Directions

Step 1
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water, then drain the pasta.


Step 2
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and Pecorino Romano cheese until well combined. Set aside.


Step 3
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta until crispy, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more.


Step 4
Add the hot, drained pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the pancetta and garlic mixture. Remove from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture into the pasta, tossing rapidly to ensure the eggs donâ€™t scramble. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until the sauce reaches the desired consistency.


Step 5
Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Pecorino Romano if desired.
